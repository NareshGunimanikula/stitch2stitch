Company Overview

The Company stitch 2 stitch is manufacturing special high quality industrial sewing machines since 2005 and selling them in 15 countries all over the world. Over the years we acquire a very large expertise in the field of sewing machine and accessories. The company has always looked to create new and better products. The development of new models and applications has grown over the last years and we have also integrated our machine in fully automatic robotised sewing systems for finishing heavy textiles such as carpets.

Who we are

All over machines are developed, programmed, produced in our own factory. The parts are made out of the best quality steels and other metals and are cut out on high precision fully automatic CNC-machines. New products and solutions for automatization are coming out yearly. Please consult us, if you consider automatization of your finishing lines.

If you need a special stitch, do not hesitate to contact us, we can surly help you. We have a library of special samples, which were made over the years, and we are still developing together with some customer, tailor-made sewing solutions which are unique.
